Plotted posters

Lately, I have been using a pen plotter to make experimental posters playing around with image and typography. The pen plotter can create interesting textures that can't be created with other printing techniques. If you're interested in one, send me an e-mail!


Face recognition-blocking sunglasses

This short video showcases different pairs of sunglasses designed to block face recognition technology by reflecting and blocking infrared light. It also shows instructions on how to make these sunglasses yourself.

Sunglasses and video, 2021
