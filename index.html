Internet Censorship 2009—2023Info

This is a book based on a dataset about internet censorship. Every page contains an instance of a country blocking a particular website, or shutting down the whole internet. The information is formatted like a punch card, where the numbers form a unique pattern based on the provided data. Per case, the colour of the cards is darker the longer the internet was blocked.

Book design, Digital + RISO printed, 2023

Generative LetterpressInfo

Letterpress printing is one of the oldest forms of printing, but it is quite obscure today. With this method, a design’s white space is created by invisible elements pushing the printed elements into place. I created a methodology using textured geometric elements and randomly chosen words to eventually form a continuously changing pattern. The text forms sentences that change meaning depending on the configuration of the words. The pattern forms using friction and compression, which makes it it quite hard to calculate or create with a computer.

Poster series, letterpress, 2023
